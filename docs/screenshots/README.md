# Report pages overview

Below are key pages of the Power BI report with a short explanation of analytical purpose and logic.

---

## 01. Finance overview
![Finance overview](01_finance_overview.jpg)

High-level financial dashboard showing:
- total revenue
- gross profit
- ROI
- core cost components (commission, logistics, cost of goods)

Used for quick assessment of overall business performance.

---

## 02. MoM leaders
![MoM leaders](02_mom_leaders.jpg)

Month-over-Month analysis identifying:
- products with the largest revenue growth
- products with the largest revenue decline

Uses calendar logic and ranking measures to compare current vs previous month.

---

## 03. Logistics ROI by warehouse
![Logistics ROI by warehouse](03_logistics_roi_by_warehouse.jpg)

Warehouse-level analysis showing:
- revenue distribution
- logistics costs
- gross profit
- ROI by WB warehouse

Used to evaluate efficiency of regional logistics and identify unprofitable locations.

---

## 04. Drillthrough SKU
![Drillthrough SKU](04_drillthrough_sku.jpg)

Detailed drillthrough page for SKU-level analysis:
- opened from summary pages
- shows full breakdown for a selected product
- supports root-cause analysis of growth or decline
